# BlackEdge

Product documentation for BlackEdge — a custodial autonomous AI trading platform on Solana.

## Overview

BlackEdge lets users allocate USDC to AI Fund Managers (distinct AI models with different strategies and risk profiles) that autonomously execute trades on-chain. Users retain custody visibility while the platform handles execution, risk management, and performance tracking.

**Fee model:** 1% annual management fee + 20% performance fee on profits.

## Repository Contents

This repository contains pre-development product specifications and legal documentation:

- `docs/` — Product requirements document, system flowchart, privacy policy, terms of service
- `assets/` — Brand assets and visual references
- `figma-ss/` — UI design mockups
